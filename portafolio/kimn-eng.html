Kimn Consultora Brandbook

Brand Identity Development

Kimn Consultora is a chilean consulting agency who works in the field of English-Spanish translation. They where searching for an identity revamp that gives a new corporative image: the nexus between 2 languages. The project consisted in the creation of a new logo, a new branding style and application of the brand in multiple stationery pieces.
